PHASE 4 — HONESTY GUARDS (anti-phantom-edge)  (read first, then execute)
================================================================================
Date: 2026-06-27 · From: Ben
Builds on the audit + PLAN. Phases 1-3 + the TAB second book are shipped — DO NOT
touch the sim, weather, promo/staking, or CLV code. Keep all tests green; one
commit at the end.

PROBLEM: the VALUE tag fires on a fixed edge+prob gate (`classify_leg`). When the
engine prices many props a round, a chunk will clear that bar by chance even with
no real edge (scan 100 props at a 5% test and ~5 flag by luck). Add discipline so
VALUE means "survives scrutiny", not "point estimate looked good".

This only gates the VALUE tag and adds an honesty caveat — it does NOT change the
sim, the ladder structure, or the prices.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GUARD 1 — Noise floor (edge must beat Monte-Carlo error)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A leg's model prob is a Monte-Carlo estimate with known standard error
(`pricing/edge.mc_standard_error`). Require the model's edge over the market to
exceed its own noise before it can be VALUE: e.g. model_prob - devig_prob >
z * mc_standard_error(model_prob, n_sims), with z configurable (default ~1.0).
A flag inside the MC noise band is not evidence of edge.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GUARD 2 — Efficiency-scaled edge threshold
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Thin, lightly-modelled props should need a BIGGER edge than liquid markets.
Required edge = VALUE_MIN_EDGE + k * (1 - liquidity_score), k configurable.
liquidity_score is a simple proxy you can build from what's available:
  - priced by 2 books (Sportsbet + TAB) vs 1  -> higher,
  - market type (H2H/line high; disposals/marks mid; goals/exotics low),
  - tighter two-way devig overround -> higher.
Keep it transparent and in config; conservative defaults.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GUARD 3 — Multiple-testing (FDR) across the round's flags
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Turn each candidate VALUE leg's noise-floor z-score into a one-sided p-value, then
apply the Benjamini-Hochberg procedure across all candidates in the round
(per prop type) at a target FDR (default 10%). Only legs that survive BH keep the
VALUE tag; the rest downgrade to SKIP (still shown, just not flagged/sized).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GUARD 4 — Honest minimum-detectable-edge caveat
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Where a flag rests on a noisy estimate, show the minimum detectable edge for the
current n_sims next to it (reuse the Phase 3 MDE helper if it fits), so a thin or
borderline flag is visibly caveated rather than presented as a clean signal.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
