below it:

    $1.50, and likewise for $3 / $5.
  - Keep one-combo-per-rung de-dup and the "every match gets a full ladder"
    guarantee (fill remaining bands from the closest leftover combo, tagged
    model-only as today).
  - Do NOT clamp the displayed probability/odds (price_shrink stays 0.0). The
    Joint prob / Fair odds columns must remain the combo's REAL values.
